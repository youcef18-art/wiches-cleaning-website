About Wiches Cleaning

Your trusted partner for exceptional cleaning services

Our Story

Founded in Ottawa-Gatineau, Wiches Cleaning has been serving the community with dedication and excellence for years. What started as a small family-owned business has grown into a trusted name in professional cleaning services.

We believe that a clean space is essential for health, productivity, and peace of mind. Our mission is to provide exceptional cleaning services that exceed expectations while building lasting relationships with our clients.
