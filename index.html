Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

No, we bring all necessary cleaning supplies and equipment. However, if you prefer us to use specific products, we're happy to accommodate your request.

How long does a typical cleaning take?

A basic clean typically takes 2-3 hours, while a deep clean can take 4-6 hours depending on the size of your property and its condition.

Are you insured and bonded?

Yes, Wiches Cleaning is fully insured and bonded for your peace of mind. All our team members are background-checked professionals.

Can I schedule recurring cleaning services?

Absolutely! We offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly recurring services at discounted rates. Contact us to set up a custom schedule.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve all areas of Ottawa and Gatineau, including surrounding communities. Contact us to confirm service in your specific location.
